Introduction

Hello there! Throughout this page we are going to explore the city of copenhagen and it's residents, based on their age, working status, city district, education and ethnicity.

The data used in this webpage is retrieved from opendata.dk. We're going to learn about the differences that are within the city of copenhagen through exploratory analysis of this data.
