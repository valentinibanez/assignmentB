Introduction

Hello there! Throughout this page we are going to explore the city of Copenhagen and it's residents based on their age, city district, income, sex, etc.

The data used in this webpage is retrieved from opendata.dk. You can head there and find even more data about Copenhagen and Denmark. We're going to learn about the differences that are within the city of Copenhagen through exploratory analysis and of course a lot of cool graphs to make it easy to swallow.

The main dataset we're using consists of 4 features with categorical values. You can check out the different values in the following table.
